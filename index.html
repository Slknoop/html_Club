Our mission is to enhance the learning and enjoyment of Computer Science and Informatics students at Indiana University South Bend. We compete in hackathons and meet together a couple times a week to discuss computing and related topics. Usually we do a Kattis problem to start the meeting and spend the rest of the time helping each other with individual interests and club goals.
